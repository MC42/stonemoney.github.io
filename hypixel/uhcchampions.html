UHC Champions

UHC Champions is Hypixel’s take on ultra-hardcore. Ultra-hardcore is a pvp game mode where there is no natural life regeneration, and the only way to gain heath is by golden apples and potions. Along with being a newly generated world the game has custom crafting recipes along with other modifications like perks, double the normal heath, and kits. It is recommended for this game to have 2 other friends that are available to play with because at the moment there is only a team of 3 mode. Once the game starts you will have 10 minutes of no pvp, this does not mean you are invincible, you can still take damage by monsters or falling (Another thing to note is that you also get 8 minutes of fire resistance). At the end of 60 minutes the remaining survivors get transported to a death-match arena, last team alive wins. Every kill and win grants points, each point you gain gets you closer to the next star rank. Star ranks are cosmetic, but show how skilled you are at the game and can get you into teams if you have a higher level.

Professions

Professions are skill trees which give you a stronger effect for each upgraded level, and every few levels grants you a new custom crafting recipe

Crafting Recipes

Custom crafting recipes are special recipes designed to make UHC champions faster paced and more insane. You unlock crafting recipes by advancing your profession skill trees. Most common custom recipes allow you to make the item up to 3 times per game, but ultimate crafting recipes can only be made once per game.
