Quakecraft

Quakecraft is a fast paced “fps” where you aim your railgun to fire a perfectly straight beam in attempts to explode your enemies, be careful as you need to take time to recharge after each shot.

Games

Games start with 7-12 players scattered around the map, they must use their railguns to kill each other. The goal of the game is to be the first player to 25 kills in solo mode or the first team to 100 kills in teams. Quakecraft is a very fast-paced game and requires a low ping and good reaction time to perform well. A large game-changer is the triggers you can unlock in the store, these triggers reduce (or extend) the time it takes to reload your gun.

Teams mode

In teams mode you can pick 1 of 2 teams, red and blue, first team to 100 kills wins. At the moment there is only one map, Quantum, as pictured above power-ups are enabled in this map.
