Blitz Survival Games

Blitz survival games is Hypixel’s take on the survival games mode. Instead of focusing on spreading out, collecting loot, and hiding, it focuses on quick swift kills and gear filled kits. 5 Minutes after the game begins, the blitz star spawns in a random chest, find it and you can unleash its power!

Kits

Kits are the most powerful mechanic in Blitz, each kit can be upgraded in the shop to improve it up to 10 levels. Premium or Advanced kits require a hefty amount of coins to unlock, and also a network level requirement (players in the pre EULA will not need to unlock the kits). Getting a level 10 kit is a great accomplishment, and takes a lot of coins. At the start of a game you can use your bow to select a kit when you right-click it, you then receive your items 30 seconds after the game begins. Upgrading kits will cost coins for each level, every level the price will increase more and more but also allow you to get more items and armor upon each level increase.

Blitz Star

5 minutes after the game begins the blitz star spawns in a random chest on the map. Since most of the chests found in the map are at the center (or start) most people look there first. Once you have the blitz star you can right click it and choose a power to activate. Each one gives a brief description when you’re in the menu but this chart below can give you a better idea about what does what.

Solo

Solo is a free for all no teaming version of blitz, it has many more maps then teams does. Most of the time solo maps are smaller than teams, or have less players due to the popularity of teams.

Teams

A newer mode where teaming is allowed, but you need to be on the same team at the start of the game. That means no cross-teaming! To be on the same team at the start of the game use the /team command or join the game while in a party of 2-3.
