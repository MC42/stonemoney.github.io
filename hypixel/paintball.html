Paintball

Paintball is one of the first games to ever come out on the Hypixel Network, and has stayed faithful to what it was years ago today. Every game starts out with 120 lives (or more if you have the godfather perk) and the goal is to make the other team run out of lives before you do. There are also special killstreak perks that change the game giving you triple shot, super throw (throw farther), and extra lives.
