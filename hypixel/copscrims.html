Cops vs Crims

Cops vs Crims is a Minecraft version of Counter Strike, using a custom resource pack it has guns, radio commands, custom armor, and custom paintings that work as signs to point you in the right direction. When you start a game, you have a shop menu in the 9th slot in your hotbar, if you right click it you can see at the guns and armor you can buy at a later point with game money. You can get game money when killing an enemy, and winning a round (you also get a small amount for losing.)

TIP: Each gun has a different set damage, range, recoil, and reload time. You should try each gun starting out to get a good feel at what will fit for you.

The goal in the game varies if you are a cop, or criminal, you can tell by typing in chat or looking at your objective in the sidebar. As a cop you must kill all the criminals or defuse the bomb planted at the A or B points on each map. As a criminal you must plant the bomb at those A and B points, and protect it from the cops trying to defuse it. After 4 rounds you swap roles and do the opposite of your original task (cops are now the criminals, ect.) The first team to get 5 points (or round wins) wins the game, but you can tie the game if both teams get 4 points. Also in the shop you can buy assorted grenades, here is a video displaying an effect of what each grenade does

In the lobby each gun can be upgraded using coins for less recoil, less cost, and more damage, you can also upgrade your character for chances at double the coins for a kill, less of a speed debuff holding weapons, and more starting money. VIP ranks can get special cosmetic textures for guns, and armor that can be bought in the lobby shop menu. VIP ranks can also get special callouts and taunts from the shop, and MVP+ ranks can get sprays.
