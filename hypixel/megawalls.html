Mega Walls

Mega Walls is a largely expanded version of the walls, with a large array of pvp mechanics. Instead of some basic starting kit items there are now classes that all have their own abilities and powers. There are two types of classes, normal classes and hero classes, hero classes cost more coins to unlock and get upgrades.

Gameplay

In Mega Walls you have 10 minutes to prepare for battle when the walls fall. In these 10 minutes it is a good idea to collect and smelt some iron so you can make multiple sets of armor so that you are prepared to fight when you respawn. Yes, you can respawn in this game, as long as your wither is still alive.

TACTICS

There are some tactics and strategies some people/teams use to outsmart their enemies and win.

Rushing

A group of people charge into a base in attempts to kill or severely damage the wither inside it. This tactic works best when there are no defenders at the base they attack.

‘Hunger Clutch’

This tactic involves collecting as much food as possible before deathmatch begins, then camping out somewhere outside the center area. Since you receive the hunger debuff when you’re outside the safe area, you need lots of food to stay alive. People will try to find and attack you but if they don’t have enough food to make the trip, they will die, or if they find you they will be weak. This tactic does not work in practice mode.
