Skywars

Skywars is a quick game where you faceoff with 11, 22, or 95 other players depending on the mode you select. The goal is simple, be the last man alive to win and never fall into the void, or else game over man! In the middle of the map is a larger island with better more powerful loot. It’s recommended that players rush the middle in hopes of controlling it and winning the game. When a game takes too long, the chests refill twice then an event called doom starts. In the “Doom” event a dragon spawns and starts destroying the islands, over time more and more dragons spawn until there is one survivor left or until the time runs out and the game ends with a stalemate.

Insane Mode

Insane mode changes the items that are in your chests to be more powerful. Instead of leather, gold, chain, and iron armor it has only iron and diamond armor. Instead of stone swords with sharpness, there is diamond swords with sharpness, etc. Insane mode also has stronger items in the middle too, including the all-powerful slime of knockback 10. Before the game beings, vip ranks can vote on special events like a dragon, or tnt rain.

Mega Mode

In mega mode you’re on teams of 5 fighting 20 other teams, that’s a 100 player game. Chests have items similar to the insane mode item-pool, same goes for the middle except that it does not have the "all-powerful" knockback 10 slime. This mode also has upgradable kits, up to level 5.

Soul Well

In Skywars in the middle of the lobby there is a soul well, to open it you need 10 souls. Every kill is 1 soul. Once you open the soul well and activate it you can unlock perks, kits, coin bags, and cages. If you access the soul well settings you can make it spin up to 5 times at once for the same cost as 5 individual spins (50 souls.) For 1000 Hypixel credits you can make the entire lobby have “double fortune” which doubles your chances of rare and legendary items (This does not affect the mystery box.) Double Fortune is commonly activated in lobby 1.
