Arena

Arena brawl is a competitive pvp game with special abilities and skills.

Offensive Skills

Offensive skills use your energy bar (or experience level) to deal out damage, it can be activated by right-clicking your sword. Remember that each attack deals a different amount of damage and takes a different amount of energy.
Example of Offensive Skill: Fireball, Lightning, Frost Breath

Utility Skills

Utility skills are unique in which some effect the area of the arena, like barricade while others can be a buff like bull charge. Utility skills can be activated with the glowstone dust, and go on cooldown when you see the gunpowder. Each skill has its own cooldown.
TIP: bulls charge can destroy enemy structures like healing totems or barricades.

Support Skills

Healing skills are simple enough, they heal you. You can activate healing skills by right-clicking the lime green dye. Like secondary skills they also have cooldowns (shown as the grey dye) and each skill has its own timer. Unlocked by default is the healing totem which over time regenerates 900 heath in 3 batches of 300, be careful because enemies can break these totems.

Ultimate Skills

Ultimate skills are also unique, you can only use them once per game. These skills are temporary strong buffs like berserk which makes you deal double attack damage and a speed boost. Everyone gets their skills (off cooldown) at the same time. Unlocked by default is the guardian ultimate which reduces the damage you take for a short amount of time.

Other Shop Items

Runes

Runes give a rare special chance to either give extra energy, deal double damage, slow your target, or a speed boost for yourself. Each upgrade increases your chance to activate the rune.

Combat Upgrades

Combat upgrades are slight stat increases, these are not very impactful but if you have the spare coins it is worth getting. A fully upgraded person can deal 2 extra damage, have 200 more health, 20 more energy, and 10% less cooldown AFTER you spend upwards of about 1 million coins.

Swords

Swords are found in the mystery chest and are cosmetic. There are both enchanted and normal swords of all the variants (gold, diamond, ect.) The enchanted swords can not be obtained at this moment.

Arena Challenger

The arena challenger is an npc that awards you for winning multiple 2v2 games in a row, also called a streak. You can trade your streak for coins, or for keys in the mystery chest (not to be confused with mystery box/vault.) You can also buy these keys for coins, and find them in games.

Mystery Chest

(Not to be confused with mystery box, the cosmetic item system for every game)
The mystery chest is a luck based chance box that can award you with multiple rewards. Some of these rewards include: cosmetic hats, cosmetic swords, and locked or expensive skills like the star shield. Often times you will be unlucky and only get a fraction of coins (10-100 coins).

Armor

Armor in arena brawl shows your level in the 2v2 mode ranking system. It is cosmetic.
