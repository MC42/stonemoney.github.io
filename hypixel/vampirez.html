VampireZ

The ultimate battle between humans and vampires. Humans must survive 20 waves of zombies, and when a human dies, they become a vampire. The game gets harder and harder for the humans the longer they live, as more will die and become vampires which then try to kill the survivors. Each wave is shorter than the other which fills the game with zombie hordes.

Survivor

As a survivor your goal is to stay alive for 20 waves, after that the game ends and you win! Survivors have their own shop where they can exchange gold for armor, weapons, and other consumables. Gold is given out at the start of the game and each wave, and also for killing zombies and vampires. You can exchange your VampireZ coins into gold so that you can get more items quicker. When you die you become a vampire.

Vampire

As a vampire your goal is to kill all the survivors before the 20 waves are up. If every survivor is killed, you win! Vampires have their own shop where they can exchange blood for buffs and abilities. Blood is given out at the start of the game and each wave, when a survivor is killed, and other methods if you buy upgrades in the lobby. You can exchange your VampireZ coins into blood so that you can get things quicker. When you die, you respawn!

SHOP

In the shop you can buy perks that give small bonuses to gameplay, and disguises which are cosmetic. Unlike the in game shop, these are permanent.

BIG PERKS

Foresight
