Party Games

The party games are a series of even shorter minigames played back to back. To win you need the most stars at the end of the game. Each minigame awards stars for the best performance, 3 for first place, 2 for second place, and 1 for third place. Some small minigames include:

Party Games 1

Avalanche
Platforms appear above you, take cover from the avalanche of snowballs! TIP: After round 14 pvp is enabled!
Volcano
Survive as the hot lava of a volcano begins to burn the ground you stand on. Not only do blocks randomly get burned, but also the blocks you stand on!
Boat Racing
Be the first to get to the finish line on your boat, watch out for logs as they send you back to the start
Punch the Bats
Punch bats to collect points, watch for the bonus bats! TIP: You can double jump!
Animal Slaughter
Slay animals and get points, but watch out for the – (minus) points! TIP: chickens grant 1 point, pigs 3, and cows 5.
Trampolino
Jump on a trampoline and collect floating wool to get points, the boosters allow you to get the red wool! First to 50 points wins!
Hoe Hoe Hoe
Claim as much land with your diamond hoe as possible, you can only claim blocks next to your color.
Mine Cart Racing
Shoot the wool of your color to gain speed and zoom to the finish line!
Hungry, Hungry Piggy
Use your fishing rod to get pigs into your goal!

Party Games 2

Lab Escape
Use the correct tool to dig down, first one to the cake at the bottom wins!
Bombardment
Don’t blow up! TNT rains from the cannons on both sides, get out of the blast radius.
Workshop
Craft the needed item with resources you can find on your shelf, furnaces instant smelt and you instantly break blocks. Craft Quickly!
Horse Race
Race your horse around the track for 3 laps the fastest to claim victory. Easier said then done as there are obsticles in your path!
RPG-16
Fire your rocket pig grenades at enemies, don’t get hit!
Chicken Rings
You ride a chicken, your goal is to fly through the rings to the goal, and quickly!
Lawn Mooer
You’re a cow. Eat (break) as much grass as possible!
Pig Jousting
Fight your enemies atop your trusty pig, last one alive claims gold!
Shooting Range
Kill as many moving mobs as possible, first to 60 points wins the game. TIP: Mobs with armor grant more points than the regular mobs.

Party Games 3

Frozen Floor
Be the last man standing by knocking other players out using your Snow Balls! But keep an eye on the icy floor, as it might be melting under your feet!
Jigsaw Rush
You said you like puzzles? Test your abilities and speed recreating the pattern given using the different blocks provided in your inventory, be the fastest and enjoy your victory!
The Floor Is Lava
Test your concentration and your skills by keeping up a good rhythm to beat this fast-paced parkour! Make sure you don’t fall to the lava, or else you will burn to ashes!
Spider Maze
Spooky, spooky! This game will bring out your deepest fears! Beat the maze and get to the safe tower in the middle, but beware, something terrifying will be hunting you down!
Fire Leapers
You’re trapped in a room surrounded by fire and burning smoke coming at you! What to do? Take advantage of the jump boosts you have to avoid getting burned! The more successful jumps you make, the more points you will get!
Jungle Jump
Another take on fast-paced parkour! Jump up, jump down, move left and right to beat it! If you fall… well, I’ve got some bad news for you
Hole In The Wall
A wall approaches you with a shape cut out of it. Worry not! Build that shape with the blocks provided to get points. The more accurate you are, the more points you will get!
Cannon Painters
Go wild guys! Coloring a canvas with eggs is fun, so shoot as many as you can to fill the board with your color and proclaim yourself victorious!
