 Bounty Hunters

In bounty hunters you spawn with a bow, and a sword. Your bow can instantly kill any target, but be careful as it only has 1 arrow. Kills replenish heath give 1 arrow, and 1 point (or 5 if it is your bounty target.) The boss bar at the top of the screen shows your target, and the compass points towards it. Your target is surrounded in red particles and a new one is selected after time. Have the most points at the end of the game to win.
