 Farm Hunt

In the waiting lobby you can chose to spawn as either an animal or a hunter. As an animal your goal is to hide from the hunters and survive until the time runs out, as a hunter you must find and kill the hidden animals!

Animals

Animals have a lot of different things they can do to get coins or defend themselves. Using a blaze rod you can right click on another animal to transform in to that animal. Your bow can shoot hunters and knock them back far with the punch enchantment. The more hunters you hit, the higher the knockback you get. Animals also get taunts with the redstone, sugar, and fireworks. Each taunt is more risky than the next, but you also get coins for doing it!
