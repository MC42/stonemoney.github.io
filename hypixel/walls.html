The Walls

You have 15 minutes to prepare before your little chunk of the arena opens up to all the other teams. Be the last team standing to win!

Core Gameplay

You start the game in a lobby where you can pick your team either Red, Blue, Green, or Yellow. Once there is enough players to begin the game starts. When the game starts you get 15 minutes of preparation time before the walls fall down and open your teams fourth of the arena to all the other teams, and the middle section. In the middle section you can find potions or other supplies in the branches off the center and in the center itself has powerful gear and diamonds to mine. The game ends once one team is left alive, or until time runs out (~45 mins)

Maps

Most maps in the walls consist of 4 large identical sections (except for a few different colors or orientations) that each team starts in. Most maps have hidden chests and loot for you to find in these sections, but the best loot is found in the middle. The middle area has loot such as potions, diamonds, and weapons and is blocked off by the walls for the first 15 minutes, many teams try to rush the middle as soon as the walls fall to attempt to get these items.

Unlocks

In walls you can unlock starting items and perks to help you in the future games. Starting items is exactly as it sounds, items that you instantly get at the start of the game. In the shop emerald you can unlock different starting items with coins as well as upgrading those starting items. Perks are different, these are triggered at certain events and certain actions in the game such as the walls falling or getting a kill. An example of a perk is vampire, when you get a kill you receive a short regeneration buff. Most perks can be upgraded to last longer or have stronger effects.
