Arcade

Arcade is a collection of smaller games into one category. Most arcade games last less than 10 minutes, unlike other games arcade does not have any upgrades. Instead you use arcade coins to exchange them into other game coins, there is a limit of 3 per day.


Arcade splits up games into the following:

  • Party Games
  • Build Battle
  • Blocking Dead
  • Bounty Hunters
  • Dragon Wars
  • Farm Hunt
  • Pixel Painters
  • Hole in the Wall
