Turbo Kart Racers

Turbo Kart Racers is an advanced minigame with a very detailed 3D model texture pack for actual 3D vehicles and items. The game uses Cubik by Sphax to deliver these detailed models and textures. In the game you drive laps around various different courses in attempts to claim first place; and there are item boxes on the field to give various power-ups and items that will boost you, and slow down your opponent. If you finish all 3 laps at the end of the game you get a random part that gives minor improvements to your racer. You can go to the mechanic in the lobby to apply them. More specifically use the part selector in the shop.
