Crazy Walls

In crazy walls there are two different sets of walls that split the arena into a grid of 16 boxes. Each player or team gets their own section just like in the walls. The green walls fall first, and then a couple minutes later the red walls fall. The game goes at a much quicker pace than The Walls and seems more like Skywars.

You probably will not be crafting very much in this game as Crazy Walls has an in-game shop villager that you can trade your resources for items. The shop inventory is different in each mode (Lucky and Normal). Golden Skulls have a chance of appearing after you kill a player or win the game, these skulls can be used at the giant zombie in the lobby to unlock new perks and kits. This is similar to the soul well from Skywars except in this there is a special “Golden Dust” currency for when you get a repeat item. The golden dust does not have a use at the moment

Besides the normal mode there is also a lucky mode. In this mode you have some changed mechanics that make crazy walls even crazier.

  • Different Resources to mine
  • Different perks and kits
  • Spawning with iron armor and iron tools
  • Lucky blocks
  • Super Lucky Blocks found in the center

Lucky blocks can be found when mining and can also be bought in the in-game shop for 1 player head (or kill), the items found in shop lucky blocks can drastically change the game! For example, you can get an axe that strikes down lightning bolts!
